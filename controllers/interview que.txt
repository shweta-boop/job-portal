==== kafka interview question==
Q: Why Kafka?
Async processing, decoupled services, high throughput
Q: Partition?
Parallel consumption
Q: What is consumer group?
Group of consumers where Kafka ensures each message is processed by only one consumer.Load balancing.

Identifier of application connecting to Kafka.
que: groupId?
One message → only ONE consumer in group gets it

Using broker list provided in config.

Last read message
